# digital-writing-folio
Welcome to my digital writing folio!
# Introduction 
I write poetry, short stories, essays, creative non-fiction — but mostly, I blend between all genres. I like to not only read, but also to *write* between the lines. 

To write, I use a journal and a pen most commonly, while reflecting, developing, and observing. It makes me feel like a hands-on detective investigating the world around me. When I write in a book, there’s no editing, or need for it to be anything. It just is, and it can just be for me. The involvement of technology in my work (usually) comes in later stages where I digitise, and bring a fine, editing brush to things as it’s quicker to type and I can see, move and contain things around in the appropriate ways. 

However, I am interested and curious in how the digital environment can be used as a generation tool for a whole new way of expressing — how what has usually felt constraining could be nurturing, a place for new currently unknown possibilities of form.
